# V4 - HONEST LIMITATION: frozen baseline protects the DEPOSIT, not trailing PROFIT
################################################################################################################
  On the real stream the account grows to $24688 (profit $+19688); the breaker
  baseline stays FROZEN at $5000. The largest peak-to-trough give-back of realized equity
  was $1226 = 4.8% of peak equity -- tame -- but the 5%/10% breaker
  does NOT act on it, because drawdown is measured vs the frozen $5000, not vs the peak.
  STARK CONSEQUENCE: with the baseline frozen at the deposit, the account could in theory give back
  ALL profit -- from $24688 down to $4500 (~$20188) -- before HALT fires.
  So the breaker is a DEPOSIT protector (ruin-proof on capital) but NOT a PROFIT protector. If
  locking banked gains matters, that is a SEPARATE high-water-mark / trailing-equity breaker -- a
  Bill WHAT (propose only; note the tradeoff: a trailing halt can whipsaw-stop a volatile-but-
  profitable strategy, so it needs its own tuning + CIATS ownership). Not added here.

VERDICT - drawdown breakers VALIDATED (with one honest design caveat for Bill)
================================================================================================================
  1. RUIN IS STRUCTURALLY PREVENTED. The G7 breaker is a capital-preservation floor: accumulated
     trading cannot take the account below ~90% of the DEPOSIT without a mandatory human-review HALT.
     Confirmed on the real 7.5yr stream + 300 pair-resamples (HALT tripped 0 times; worst deposit
     loss 1.3-4.9%). The breaker works as designed.
  2. THE TB00802a T6 'FAIL' IS RESOLVED -- it was a DENOMINATOR ERROR: $518 vs a $500 concurrent-
     margin base. Against the real $5000/module deposit the worst loss was 1.3%, and the peak-to-
     trough give-back was 4.8% of equity. The strategy is well within survivable.
  3. ACCOUNT SIZING: $5000/module (registry) is comfortable at $50/clip (0 pauses; 1.3% worst).
     Even $1500 survives (0 HALTs); below ~$1500 the breaker protects capital but PAUSEs more often
     (undersized for $50 clips). Recommend >= $5000/module, or scale the clip to the deposit.
  4. ONE HONEST CAVEAT (V4): the FROZEN baseline protects the DEPOSIT, not trailing PROFIT -- in
     theory the account could give back all gains to ~90% of deposit before HALT. Whether to add a
     high-water-mark trailing breaker is a Bill WHAT (propose only; has its own whipsaw tradeoff).
